About me

I’m Jaime Yoon (Jumi), a product designer and graduate student living in San Francisco, originally from Korea. With a mixed background of Interior design, Web design, User Experience design. I create, evaluate, and improve designs through the user research and principles of design. I'd love to work with various people with different background. When I'm not designing, I spend my time cooking, traveling, going camping (Redwood state park is my favorite places to go) and watching SF movies. I am also passionate about coding, illustration and learning new things. Feel free to contact me!
