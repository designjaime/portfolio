    INTRODUCTION

    Subaru US project called “Subaru Voyage” Subaru customers can travel from coast to coast past each Subaru summit. Each summit has a car with a unique design and offers a variety of options for customers to enjoy nature. Our Motive experience center is located in Portland, Oregon.

    INSPIRATION AND APPLICATIONS

    We think "Unlock your potential" is a core value of lifestyle in 2030'. How will ACTIVE LIFE STYLE evolve in 2030? We figured out which touch point can be success especially when users do outdoor activities. We found a solution through the difficulties of the non-subaru owner activity. As results, Subaru owners can enjoy their outdoor activities without any concerns of run out of the battery and also he lives active lifestyle motivated by his environment that Subaru providing seamless experience from work, activities to outdoor.

    TARGET AUDIENCE

    Based upon data from in-person interviews, surveys, I organized my observations and categorized them using an affinity map. They think their pets as a family member and willing to take a risk to save their pets in case of emergency, also not only their pets but also would to like save the neighbor’s pet. Pet owners worry about their pet threaten by house fire or gas leak. According to information, We can narrow down features that need most.

    USER JOURNEY

    The user journey can tell how pet owners are interacting with the services or products they are currently using. And it help us find when and what our services and products are needed to our target audiences.
