Coqueta

2017
Task: Branding + UX/UI Design, Visual Design

Design Practice
Spanish Tapas Restaurant Rebranding and UX/UI design for website/interactive menu

Coqueta is a spanish restaurant at pier 5 in San Francisco. Coqueta offers a excellent modern Interpretation of Spanish Tapas, wine and inspired cocktails with beautiful view from the pier. They’re targeting the local foodies, tourists. Restuarant try to flirt with their customers with special food, drinks and beautiful view of pier. Logo represent identity of restaurant that try to flirt customers.

Scope of work : Brand identity, Website design, Interactive menu design, Colletral design
