Social platform app + digital signage for event

Emento is an social platform application based on using digital signage for events. We believe signage becomes more personalized experience and more on demanded sharing in real-time. And also it is new powerful way of advertisement.

My job : UX/UI design, Prototype, User Research, Wireframe



Demo video

Problems

There are lots of social media and people want to share their moments with friends and family, but it’s hard to share photos, videos privately on each event with a group of people.


Solutions

1) Private social platform for any events
2) Selective sharing of messages, photos, and live video streaming
3)Home TV becomes interactive live-wall
