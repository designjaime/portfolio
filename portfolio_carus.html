    • THE CHALLENGE

    • Seperated time
    • Bored and tired time
    • Limited activities

    Long road trips with kids aren't always fun fests. But they are doable, and at times even enjoyable. How to improve the experience of driving with kids, especially long-distance travel?. It is not only for the family member but also for the young adults who want to go on a road trip with friends. Apps can interact with cars and people on the go, helping to make long distance travel with family fun. The app using autonomous sensor technology that makes every traveler participate with random quizzes such as related to the local place where pass by or simple trivia game.

    USER SCENARIO

    USER STORY A

    Sarah and Her husband Jack

    • • The children complain and tantrum is stressing.
    • • They want quiet and peaceful time on her way.
    • • They feel angry with their children and ruin their family trips.
    • • Her husband Jack is annoyed by the loud voice of the children.

    Sarah and her family plan to travel this summer. She has a 6-years old son and a 10-years old daughter. Her family tried a long trip a year ago, but her family felt stressed while on the road. This year her family is going to Grand Canyon from San Francisco, which will take almost 10 hours by car. Her friend suggests the CarUs app and she wants to try out this application. When they are going to go to travel, Sarah downloaded the application and she was able to connect the application to CarPlay via Bluetooth. When they left for about two hours, kids start to complain and get bored. Sarah and her husband were annoyed and tired. Sarah suggests play the game with CarUs, and the whole family starts to share their playlists before they participate game.

    USER STORY B

    Kimberly and Ryan

    • • Kimberly and Ryan get bored and tired.
    • • Playing the video game brings motion sickness.
    • • Ryan wants to eat the sandwich for lunch.
    • • Kimberly wants to play with mom and brother.

    Kimberly and Ryan were excited to go to travel with their parents, but they always felt bored and tired on the road. This time they brought devices but after a few hours, without wi-fi, there were many things to do. Their mom suggested playing the new game with CarUs application. Kimberly pick her favorite song and send to CarPlay, Ryan want to eat the sandwich for lunch, he picks Subway for next stop.

    PROOF OF CONCEPT

    CONCLUSION

    Using autonomous technology, apps can be useful when users travel long distances in a car. They can spend quality time with family or friends on the road. Parents do not always have to help children and children can interact with the environment or things they pass by. Through this experience, the app can provide knowledge to children and have fun in a boring time. Give a child a reward, the children and parents will become more engaged. The challenge is to design an interface that is easy for young children to use. Because considering work with different platforms from CarPlay screen, mobile and tablet device should makes smooth user flow. I’ll test with first version of design, and make iteration for next step.
