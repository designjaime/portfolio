1. Photography

Totoro Ramen restaurant located in San Mateo, California. They are mainly serving authentic Japanese ramen and other appetizers. This new restaurant needs a new brand or character for marketing and interior design. The client wants the cartoon style that friendly and cute as they named restaurant "Totoro" but not related to Japanese animation. "Totoro" actually comes from mispronouncing Tororu, which means troll in Japanese. Based on that, I created a character inspired by a traditional Japanese lucky cat and troll. I came up with ideas that combined with the shape of noodles and cat. Based on the new brand, I was designed menu, website, business card, etc.

2. Brand Identity

Based on the meaning of Totoro, I start to create a brand that troll or cat is eating ramen. The shape of the noodle is unstructured and smooth, so I draw a character with freehand style not structured. Emotions of the brand are cute, freedom, friendly, kind and pleasant.
