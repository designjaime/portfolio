Totoro Ramen

Totoro Ramen restaurant is located in San mateo, California and they are serving traditional noodle dishes. The restuarant is targeted young people who loves ramen. I created character inspired by traditional japanese lucky cat.
The restuarant atmosphere will be fun and energetic.

Key words : Comfort food, Ramen, Modern, Trendy, Fun, Quick, Interesting, Totoro(means Troll in Japanese), Authentic

Scope of work : Brand identity, Interactive menu design, Collateral design




Key elements : Bowl, Chopstick, Cat, Noodle, Brush
