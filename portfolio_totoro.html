Totoro Ramen restaurant is located in San Mateo, California. They are serving authentic Japanese ramen and more. This new restaurant needs a new brand and character for designing their restaurant. The client wants the cartoon style that friendly and cute. I created a character inspired by a traditional Japanese lucky cat and combined with the shape of noodles. Based on our new brand, I was designed menu, website, business card, etc.
