Totoro Ramen

Digital, Print design and Front-End Development of Japanese Ramen Restaurant.

Type: UX/UI Design + Branding + Front-End Development + Photography
My Role: I mainly focused on the branding and UX/UI design of tablet menu, website. I also developed their website and analyzed the data used Google Analytics.

The Story

The client wants the cartoon style that friendly and cute as they named restaurant "Totoro " but not related to Japanese animation. "Totoro " actually comes from mispronouncing Tororu, which means troll in Japanese. Based on that, I created a character inspired by a traditional Japanese lucky cat and troll. I came up with ideas that combined with the shape of noodles and cat. Based on the new brand, I was designed menu, website, business card, etc.

Brand Identity

Based on the meaning of Totoro, I start to create a brand that troll or cat is eating ramen. The shape of the noodle is unstructured and smooth, so I draw a character with freehand style not structured. Emotions of the brand are cute, freedom, friendly, kind and pleasant.

Key elements : Bowl, Chopstick, Cat, Troll, Noodle, Brush, Pen art

Visual Design

UX/UI Design: Tablet menu

Using Adobe XD, Easy to deliver the design specs to developer. Developers can see detailed design specs through the link.
