FRONT-END DEVELOPMENT

I have the same process for every design work. First, define the problems that what is the problem, why we need a new solution and who's going to use it. Next, Suggest solutions or set the goal. In this process, we need to communicate with the client, users for iterate designs. There are lots of methods for testing. I preferred to meet the person face to face and interview them see how they use it. Finally, make them visually delightful for target users. I think every project has a different target audience, solution and designers should know what makes a good experience for users.
